Privacy Policy

Blink is a simple journaling app designed to help you capture one line a day. We value your privacy and collect as little data as possible.

What we store

  • Your entries, moods, and optional photos are stored locally on your device.
  • If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored securely in your private iCloud account via CloudKit.

What we don’t do

  • We don’t run ads or use third-party analytics/tracking SDKs.
  • We don’t have access to your journal data.
  • We don’t process payments—Apple handles purchases via the App Store.

Notifications

Daily reminders are local notifications on your device. No notification data leaves your device.

Your control

  • You can delete entries and summaries at any time.
  • Deleting the app removes locally stored data. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can manage synced data in iCloud settings.
