Support

We're here to help. Most issues can be solved quickly with the steps below.

Contact

When writing, please include your device model and iOS version.

Purchases & Premium

  • To restore a purchase, open Blink → Insights → Unlock → Restore Purchases.
  • If restore fails, ensure you’re signed into the same Apple ID used to buy Premium.

Reminders

  • Enable notifications in iOS Settings → Notifications → Blink.
  • In Blink, pick your reminder time in Settings.

iCloud Sync

  • Make sure iCloud Drive is enabled and you are signed in.
  • Toggle iCloud sync in Blink’s Settings.
